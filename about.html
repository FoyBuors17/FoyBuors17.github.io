About Me
Hi, I'm Lesia Buors — founder and hands-on owner of Tidy Fox Cleaning Services.

Before starting Tidy Fox, I spent over 20 years working in customer service and the cleaning industry, including several management roles. I've always had a passion for helping people, and one of the things I enjoyed most was building real connections with clients. Starting my own company was a natural next step — I wanted the freedom to do things my way, with honesty, integrity, and attention to detail leading the way.

For me, cleaning isn't just about tidying up a space. There's something deeply satisfying about turning an untidy home into a place that feels calm and refreshed. I love knowing that when a client walks through their front door, they'll feel that same sense of relief and joy. I've been there — balancing work, family, and trying to keep up with a house — and I know how much of a difference a clean home can make.

What sets Tidy Fox apart is the personal care we put into every home we service. I still visit clients myself, pitch in on cleanings, and make the effort to truly get to know the families we work with. To me, our clients aren't just appointments on a schedule — they're people we care about. I take pride in offering a service that's not only reliable and consistent but also deeply rooted in trust.

I'm proud to lead a hardworking, friendly, and detail-oriented team that shares my values. And I'm even prouder to have built a business where clients feel looked after, supported, and free to enjoy more time doing what they love.

Thank you for being part of the Tidy Fox family — and for trusting us in your home.

— Lesia
