Regular Clean

Our cleaning checklist is designed to be as thorough as it gets! Every nook, corner and cranny is checked to make sure that your home receives a thorough and effective cleaning by your Tidy Fox Cleaning team.



Kitchen

  • Polish exterior of all appliances
  • Microwave inside and outside
  • Rangehood (exterior)
  • Countertop
  • Backsplash (window and ledge)
  • Polish faucet
  • Polish sink
  • Light clean of cabinets (exterior)
  • Cabinet fixtures
  • Tables/bar tops/chairs
  • Dust all surfaces
  • Sweep and wash floors
  • Clean and put away dishes
  • Empty trash

Bathrooms

  • Vanity Countertops
  • Vanity Doors
  • Vanity fixtures
  • Clean mirror(s)
  • Polish sink(s)
  • Polish faucet
  • Clean tub/shower tiles
  • Shower glass
  • Disinfect toilets
  • Sweep and wash floors
  • Dust shelves
  • Dust light fixture above mirror
  • Empty trash

Bedrooms

  • Make beds
  • Dust exposed surfaces
  • Dust and wipe lamps
  • Dust picture frames
  • Clean mirrors
  • Vacuum/sweep and wash floors
  • Basic organization (5 minutes)
  • Empty trash

All Other Common Areas

  • Couch and cushions vacuumed
  • Arrange cushions
  • Arrange throw blankets
  • Dust all table tops and decor
  • Vacuum/sweep and wash floors
  • Basic organization (5 minutes)
  • Washer and dryer exteriors
  • Banister, handrails dusted
  • Empty trash

Move In/Out Clean

Our Move In/Out Clean is a more detailed clean for empty homes that need extra attention before moving in, selling, or finishing a lease. It goes beyond our regular checklist by focusing on built-up dust, inside appliances, cupboards, and the areas that matter most before a move-out inspection.



Kitchen

  • Clean exterior and interior of all appliances
  • Microwave inside and outside
  • Rangehood
  • Countertop cleaned and wiped down
  • Backsplash cleaned and wiped down
  • Polish faucet
  • Polish sink
  • Interior and exterior wipe down of all cabinets
  • Cabinet fixtures cleaned and wiped down
  • Dust all surfaces
  • Sweep and wash floors
  • Empty trash

Bathrooms

  • Vanity Doors and inside cabinets
  • Vanity fixtures

  • Dust shelves

  • Empty trash

Bedrooms

  • Dust exposed surfaces
  • Clean mirrors
  • Vacuum/sweep and wash floors
  • Dust and clean closets and inside cabinets
  • Empty trash

All Other Common Areas

Rental/Airbnb Clean

Turnover-focused cleaning so your rental or short-stay is guest-ready: high-touch surfaces, refreshed living spaces, and details that help you protect reviews and repeat bookings.



Kitchen

  • Sanitize high-touch surfaces (handles, switches, appliance pulls)
  • Microwave inside and outside
  • Rangehood (exterior)
  • Countertops and backsplash cleaned
  • Polish faucet and sink
  • Exterior of appliances; coffee maker wiped down
  • Tables, bar tops, and chairs wiped down
  • Floors vacuumed and washed
  • Garbage removed
  • Check and tidy kitchen presentation

Bathrooms

  • Vanity countertops cleaned
  • Clean mirrors
  • Polish sinks and faucets
  • Tub/shower cleaned
  • Toilets disinfected (including exterior and base)
  • Floors vacuumed and washed
  • Light fixture above mirror dusted
  • Garbage removed
  • Refill/check guest supplies if provided

Bedrooms

  • Beds made (or stripped if you provide linens)
  • Pillows and linens reset
  • Dust exposed surfaces, lamps, and frames
  • Clean mirrors
  • Floors vacuumed and washed
  • Closets neat; hangers aligned
  • Garbage removed
  • Guest-ready presentation check

Living Area & Finishing Touches

  • Sofa and cushions vacuumed; cushions and throws tidy
  • Dust tables, shelves, and decor
  • Vacuum furniture if needed
  • Glass doors and patio tracks spot-cleaned
  • Garbage removed
  • Final walkthrough presentation check
